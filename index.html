❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Root Canal Treatment and is it painful?

▼

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a procedure to remove infected pulp from inside the tooth, clean the canal, and seal it. At PURE DENT 32, we use advanced rotary instruments and effective anesthesia to ensure the procedure is completely painless. Most patients report feeling comfortable during and after the treatment.

How long do Dental Implants last?

▼

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. We use premium-grade titanium implants and follow strict protocols to ensure long-term success. Regular check-ups and good oral hygiene are essential for implant longevity.

What is Smile Design and how does it work?

▼

Smile Design is a comprehensive cosmetic dental procedure that enhances your smile's appearance. We use digital smile simulation to show you the expected results before starting. The process may include veneers, teeth whitening, gum contouring, and alignment corrections tailored to your facial features.

What is Cosmetic Dentistry?

▼

Cosmetic Dentistry focuses on improving the appearance of your teeth, gums, and smile. It includes procedures like teeth whitening, dental veneers, bonding, contouring, and smile makeovers. At PURE DENT 32, we combine artistry with advanced technology to create natural, beautiful results.

How do I book an appointment?

▼

You can book an appointment through multiple ways: fill out our online appointment form below, call us directly at +91 96681 79843, or message us on WhatsApp. We offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend slots to accommodate your busy schedule.

Do you offer pain-free treatments?

▼

Yes! We specialize in pain-free dentistry. We use advanced local anesthesia techniques, sedation options for anxious patients, and the latest equipment to minimize discomfort. Our team is trained to ensure every procedure is as comfortable as possible.
